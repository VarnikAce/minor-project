Review workflow

A practical path from suspicious post to evidence-based decision.

1. Extract the core claimSeparate the checkable statement from emotional framing and commentary.
2. Inspect the evidence trailLook for named experts, primary documents, dates, and quoted context.
3. Cross-check with trusted referencesOpen established fact-checkers and original sources before accepting the claim.
4. Share with contextIf the claim is misleading, explain what is wrong and provide a better source.
